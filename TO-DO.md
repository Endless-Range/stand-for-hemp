## ðŸš¨ Critical Reminders

### Every Day
- **Speed > Perfection**: Ship working features, iterate in production
- **Test on mobile**: That's where most users are
- **Document in GitHub**: Keep repo updated with progress
- **Track metrics**: Data drives decisions

### User Experience
- **Granny test**: If your grandma can't use it, simplify
- **60-second rule**: Landing to action completion in 60 seconds
- **No friction**: Every extra step loses 50% of users
- **Clear CTAs**: Always tell users exactly what to do next
